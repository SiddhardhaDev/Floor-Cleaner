Performance & Insights

Mobility & Agility

The four BO motors provide robust power for navigating various flat surfaces, making the robot ideal for indoor environments and light outdoor use. Its design allows for effective movement and navigation.

Maneuverability: Achieved through basic differential steering, allowing for precise turns and pivots to navigate obstacles effectively.

Power & Endurance

The $2600mAh$ 18650 Li-ion battery ensures extended operational periods for prolonged use.

Battery Life: Estimated $3-4$ hours under typical continuous movement, adjustable based on load and usage patterns.

Safe Charging: The TP4056 module provides vital overcharge/discharge protection, enhancing battery longevity and ensuring safe operation.

Stable Power: The MT3608 boost converter efficiently delivers a consistent $5V$, crucial for stable electronic component operation and preventing voltage fluctuations.

Water Pump Utility

The integrated mini submersible pump adds a unique dimension, enabling the robot to perform small-scale liquid transfer tasks for various applications.

Flow Rate: Ideal for low-flow applications, typically ranging from $80-120$ Liters/Hour, suitable for demonstrations or micro-irrigation systems.

Expandability & Learning

The Arduino Nano offers a flexible environment for programming diverse robot behaviors, from simple remote control to more advanced autonomous functions.

Modular Design: The MDF base and component choices facilitate easy addition of new sensors (e.g., ultrasonic, IR) and communication modules (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi) for endless possibilities and future upgrades.
